Inconsistent laboratory equipment availability in Sci-Tech Wing

Category

Academic Facilities

Priority

High Priority

Oct 24, 2023

subject Detailed Description

Since the beginning of the semester, the third-floor chemistry lab has frequently lacked basic titration kits and functioning Bunsen burners during scheduled practical hours. On multiple occasions, classes of 30+ students were expected to share only 5 working setups. This inconsistency is significantly impacting our ability to complete required experimental coursework for CHM-302. Students have attempted to reach out to the lab technicians, but the equipment shortages persist due to "unmet procurement cycles."
